**Reverse baryogenesis at endpoint of bounded spacetime.**

Where I have previously postulated variable ε₀ or α, elaboration is required:

**Vacuum collapse** is one **possible consequence** of either or both endpoint events. **Disassociation of baryonic matter** is a **possible further consequence**. **Variation in ε₀ or α** **may or may not** be implicated.

None of {endpoint event identity, vacuum collapse, matter disassociation, fundamental-constant variation} is committed at postulate level. The postulate commits only to: reverse baryogenesis at endpoint of bounded spacetime.

**Planck-geometry degradation is not invoked at postulate level.** Quantum-gravity effects may play a role within the phase-conjugate wavefront (separately treated, separately uncommitted) but are kept outside Postulate 2 to avoid unresolved quantum-gravity tensions.
