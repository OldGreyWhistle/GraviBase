### CD-OP-001

**Term:** Stream 3 methodology
**Subdivision:** OP
**Definition:** Research-program methodology under which UMF Trial S3M0S1 operates. Defines the boundaries and discipline of all analysis: confined to a defined document corpus; no adjacent literature search unless explicitly authorised (modified per CD-OP-016 corpus-currency due-diligence standing rule); honest reporting of negative findings required; significance thresholds 5σ / 4σ / 2σ–3σ; variable speed of light permanently rejected as framework element; parallel-track concepts tracked separately and not conflated with active modules.

### CD-OP-002

**Term:** Modal-status preservation
**Subdivision:** OP
**Synonyms:** Framing-bias correction #4
**Definition:** Standing-rule requirement that the modal status of statements (committed / possible / open / disclaimed, etc.) is preserved when translating engineering language to scientific language, or when integrating new corpus content into deliverables. Promoting Stephen's possibilities to commitments, his consequences to mechanisms, or his hedged predictions to firm predictions is a failure mode logged as framing-bias correction #4.
**Required action / handling:** At every integration of new content, self-check whether modal status of source has been preserved. Where overreach detected, correct and log as framing-bias correction. Reports of new findings ("positive or negative") presented neutrally without UMF-validation or UMF-invalidation framing.
**Modal status:** committed
**Source provenance:** Amendment 7 framing-bias correction #4; re-applied through Amendments 8, 9, 10
**First introduced:** Amendment 7 (26 May 2026)
