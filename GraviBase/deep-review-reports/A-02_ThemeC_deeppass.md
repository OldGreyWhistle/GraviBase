**CENTRAL STRUCTURAL FINDING — interpretational framework for quantum mechanics:**

**Bohmian mechanics is an interpretation of quantum mechanics**, not a separate physical theory. It makes the same empirical predictions as standard QM (in the quantum-equilibrium |ψ|² regime), but with distinct ontological commitments:

1. **Definite particle positions at all times** (not just upon measurement) — particles are real point-like entities following determinate trajectories
2. **Wavefunction is real physical entity** (not just calculational device) guiding particles via guidance equation
3. **Deterministic** — given initial particle positions + wavefunction, future evolution is deterministic
4. **Manifestly nonlocal** — guidance equation in configuration space gives instantaneous many-particle correlations
5. **No measurement problem / wavefunction collapse** — apparent "collapse" emerges from effective decoherence + branching of universal wavefunction

**Modern DGZ nomological framework** (Dürr-Goldstein-Zanghì 1992): treats wavefunction as **nomological** (law-like, not physical) — Born rule |ψ|² emerges from **quantum equilibrium hypothesis** combined with **equivariance** of |ψ|²-distribution under Bohmian dynamics. This is the **mainstream interpretation** of Bohmian mechanics in contemporary literature per Drezet-Amblard 2025 verbatim.

**Critical alternative perspectives** per Drezet-Amblard 2025 verbatim: pluralistic approach drawing on classical optics and hydrodynamics analogies; Bohm-Vigier programme + Valentini's relaxation of statistical equilibrium (relates to A-03 Valentini deep-pass). **Active debate within Bohmian-mechanics community** about the correct interpretation.
