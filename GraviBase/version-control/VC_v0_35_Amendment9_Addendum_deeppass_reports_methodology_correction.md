**§7.5 Periodic corpus-currency verification (new standing rule, Stephen 27 May AEST).**

> "Periodic corpus-currency verification on Stephen-supplied review lists is a routine research-hygiene activity, NOT exceptional scope-expansion. It does not require fresh scope-expansion authorisation for each cycle; it requires Stephen-supplied review lists (or equivalent direction) and follows the established per-paper deeppass-report convention for body-accessed papers. Web-search access for this purpose is scoped to corpus-currency verification on the Stephen-supplied review list and does not extend to general adjacent-literature search for new framework development. Stream 3 corpus-bounded methodology remains operative for new framework development; it does NOT prohibit corpus-currency verification for established corpus areas."
