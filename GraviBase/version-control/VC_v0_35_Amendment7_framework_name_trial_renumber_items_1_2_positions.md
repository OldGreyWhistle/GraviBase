### C. Items 1 and 2 substantive positions taken (v0.3 Research Plan round; Item 1 framing tightened in v0.4)

**Stephen direction (26 May AEST):** "I will address 2 follow-ups for Section 14 now."

#### Item 1 — Bounded-spacetime endpoint mechanism for variant fundamental constants

**Stephen verbatim:** "UMF implies bounded spacetime which implies a final collapse of spacetime and completion of the geometry (gravity) budget. If the vacuum collapses to equilibrium, we expect a change in ε₀ or α, possibly if Planck geometry were to 'degrade'. The alternative to this is reversible baryogenesis which could also align with UMF."

**Substantive content:** UMF position proposes a UMF-specific mechanism distinct from ADD (in the spirit of original option (b) framed around α_s strong-nuclear, though addressing different constants). The proposed UMF-specific mechanism specifically addresses variation in ε₀ and α (fine-structure constant). Whether it extends to α_s or to other fundamental constants remains an open question. Reversible baryogenesis is the alternative scenario, also UMF-compatible.

**Substantive UMF technical development required to specify** (Items flagged for UMF v0.7 / v2.3 source-content work):
1. Quantitative meaning of "completion of the geometry (gravity) budget" under UMF v0.7 / v2.3
2. Mechanism by which vacuum-equilibrium collapse drives ε₀ or α change (noting α = e²/4πε₀ℏc relates them)
3. What "Planck geometry degradation" denotes in UMF terms
4. How the reversible-baryogenesis alternative relates to the F-02 / F-05 asymmetry-generation class and to the five matter-creation classes
5. Explicit connection back to the α_s question that originally framed Item 1

**v0.4 framing-tightening note:** v0.3 Draft framed Stephen's position as "UMF position is option (b), extended beyond α_s to variant fundamental constants more broadly." This overstated Stephen's actual claim. Claude self-check upon "check your work" direction identified the framing overreach. v0.4 reissue tightened to "UMF position proposes a UMF-specific mechanism distinct from ADD (in the spirit of original option (b), though addressing different constants than α_s). The proposed UMF-specific mechanism specifically addresses variation in ε₀ and α (fine-structure constant); whether it extends to α_s or to other fundamental constants remains an open question." This is framing-bias correction #3 in the trial methodology audit (see §G below).

#### Item 2 — UMF-specific GW memory predictions / gravitoelectric DM demotion

**Stephen verbatim:** "I am happy to consider a form of dark matter [that] does not radiate and has no detectable particles. The gravitoelectric postulate could be demoted to an assumption given the above constraints, and it would be particularly valid if a resolution or finding can be empirically verified, or negated, so should be included as a verifiable test."

**Substantive content:** The gravitoelectric dark-matter commitment is demoted from postulate to assumption, subject to constraints (i) UMF DM does not radiate, and (ii) UMF DM has no detectable particles. UMF-specific gravitational-wave memory predictions per the Bieri 2021/2022 framework apparatus are to be developed as a verifiable test of the demoted gravitoelectric DM assumption — predictions that can be empirically verified or negated.

**Substantive UMF technical development required to specify** (Items flagged for UMF v0.7 / v2.3 source-content work):
1. Explicit GW memory signature UMF predicts under the bounded-universe / phase-conjugate-wavefront / gravitoelectric DM construction
2. How this signature distinguishes from the standard particle-DM null prediction and from competitor frameworks (F-08 Verlinde EG; F-09 HL DM-as-integration-constant; F-10 Refracted Gravity; Mashhoon non-local gravity)
3. Empirical infrastructure capable of testing the prediction (LIGO/Virgo/KAGRA O5; LISA; pulsar timing arrays NANOGrav/EPTA; Einstein Telescope; Cosmic Explorer)
4. Falsification criteria — what observable would refute the demoted gravitoelectric DM assumption

**Connection notes:** This Item 2 substantive position connects to §11.3 follow-up Item 6 (phase-conjugate wavefront to gravity-mass spatial offset mechanism) and Item 7 sub-item 6 (distinguishability matrix vs all competitor frameworks). The Bieri GW memory test is the verifiable-test programme for the demoted assumption.
